Svalbard Weather Map

Svalbardposten · digital archive 2006–2024

After Rodighiero & Daniélou · source

Legend

Read the map as a weather chart: warm systems are discourse entering the debate, cool systems are discourse leaving it.

Pressure

Clusters of co-occurring actors form pressure systems — highs are discourse warming into the debate, lows are discourse cooling out of it.

High — emerging discourse (warming)
Low — receding discourse (cooling)

Lines

Isobars ring each system, tightening where discourse intensifies; fronts mark where a warming system advances on a cooling one.

Isobars — tight = discourse intensity
Fronts — direction = discourse advancing

Stations

Each cross marks a single article; its colour encodes the time of publication, running from blue in 2006 to red in 2024.
